The Best Movies According to Wooje

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Old Boy

Made me think about what's the point of living. If we aren't free to effect changes, aren't we all living in a jail that we or others have created?

Fight Club

Made me question societal norm. Why should we be pressured by others to doing things? Let the dice fall where they may.

Love Me if You Dare

If there is someone you love, you should hold on to them.
